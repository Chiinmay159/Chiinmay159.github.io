About BharatVidya

BharatVidya.in offers structured and accessible courses on Indian history, Sanskrit, Vedic literature, archaeology and Indian Knowledge Systems (IKS), designed by scholars with decades of research experience.

Our mission is to make India's civilisational knowledge available to learners and institutions through academically rigorous, accurate and easy-to-understand content aligned with NEP 2020.

BharatVidya is a joint initiative of the Bhandarkar Oriental Research Institute (Pune) and Matra Media.
