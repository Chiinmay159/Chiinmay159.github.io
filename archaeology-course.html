Why Archaeology Matters

Archaeology works with evidence that stands firm against speculation. It reveals how humans built, lived, created and believed through the physical traces preserved in landscapes, soils, and stones.

This course provides a scientific lens to decode this evidence. Move beyond memorising dates into understanding how history is reconstructed, corrected and rediscovered.

Archaeology in India

The Indian subcontinent holds one of the richest archaeological records on Earth, extending from prehistoric landscapes to complex urban civilisations.

From Bori in Pune district to Odisha and the Upper Ganga valley — this course places India within a global narrative of human innovation, while spotlighting what makes the subcontinent extraordinary.
