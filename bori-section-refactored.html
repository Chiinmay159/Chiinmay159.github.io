Bhandarkar Oriental Research Institute Information

Established in 1917, the Bhandarkar Oriental Research Institute (BORI) is a premier center for research in Indology and Asian Studies, renowned globally for its contributions to manuscriptology and critical textual studies.

The institute undertakes a robust academic research program and is home to over 28,000 rare manuscripts and 1,53,000 rare books. BORI has published nearly 300 books and journals, including magnum opuses such as the Critical Edition of the Mahabharata, History of Dharmashastra, and the Vedic Bibliography.
