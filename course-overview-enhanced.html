Why study the Vedas today

The Vedas are among humanity's oldest surviving knowledge systems and the foundational texts of Indian civilization. An unbroken oral tradition preserved language, astronomy, mathematics, music, medicine and philosophy with astonishing precision.

This course brings that tradition into a modern, academic online format. You will explore how Vedic ideas about cosmic order, consciousness, ritual and ethics continue to inform global research and public discourse today.

Who is this course for?

  • Students of history, philosophy, Sanskrit and Indian Knowledge Systems
  • Professionals seeking strong foundational knowledge
  • Lifelong learners with deep curiosity

No prior Sanskrit required. Clear English explanations throughout.

What you will learn

The four Samhitas

  • Rigveda – hymns, deities, early philosophy
  • Yajurveda – ritual structures and social order
  • Samaveda – melodic tradition, roots of Indian music
  • Atharvaveda – healing, protection, civic insights

Brahmanas, Aranyakas & Upanishads

  • Symbolic and procedural layers of Vedic ritual
  • Shift from external practice to internal contemplation
  • Upanishadic vision of self, reality and liberation

The six Vedangas

Auxiliary disciplines that preserve and illuminate the Vedic corpus

  • Śikṣā – phonetics & recitation
  • Kalpa – procedural codes
  • Vyākaraṇa – grammar
  • Nirukta – etymology
  • Chandas – metres
  • Jyotiṣa – calendrics & astronomy
