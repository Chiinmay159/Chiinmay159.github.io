In Memoriam

Late Shri Rakesh Jhunjhunwala was more than a legendary investor. He had a deep and abiding interest in India's history and heritage, recognizing its immense value to the global community.

His profound understanding and respect for India's cultural wealth inspired his desire to invest in its accessibility and awareness for future generations. His unwavering support and generous patronage were pivotal in the inception and growth of Bharatvidya.in.
