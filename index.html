About BharatVidya

BharatVidya.in is your essential portal to India's timeless civilisational heritage.

This pioneering online learning platform offers self-paced online certificate courses on various strands of Indian history, connecting ancient wisdom with contemporary understanding.

Each course blends scholarship with clarity, building a vital bridge between tradition and modern learning.

BharatVidya is a joint initiative of the Bhandarkar Oriental Research Institute (BORI) and Matra Media & Communications Pvt. Ltd.
