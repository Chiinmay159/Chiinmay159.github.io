About BharatVidya

BharatVidya.in is your essential portal to India's timeless civilisational heritage.

This pioneering online learning platform offers self-paced online certificate courses in Indian History, Sanskrit, Vedas, Mahabharata, Temple Architecture, Indian Knowledge Systems (IKS), Ayurveda, Culture, and Archaeology.

Each course blends scholarship with clarity, building a vital bridge between ancient wisdom and contemporary understanding.

BharatVidya is a joint initiative of the Bhandarkar Oriental Research Institute (BORI) and Matra Media & Communications Pvt. Ltd.
